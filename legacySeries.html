Legacy Series Banjos

The Lemon Legacy Series represents Lemon’s interpretation of the most important banjo designs of the 20th century. Inspired by the instruments of the 1930s and 1950s, these banjos honor the tone, proportions, and visual character that defined bluegrass.
While rooted in tradition, each Legacy Series instrument benefits from modern precision, refined playability, and careful material selection — resulting in banjos that capture the spirit of the originals while exceeding their consistency and reliability.
These instruments exist to preserve tradition while moving it forward.

Legacy ’35 Models

The Legacy ’35 Series captures the look, feel, and tonal character of the banjos that defined the golden age of bluegrass in the 1930s.
Designed for power, clarity, and projection, these instruments deliver the dry, responsive tone and strong note separation associated with the most sought-after vintage banjos.
Built with modern precision and care, the Legacy ’35 Series preserves the sound of the past while offering the reliability expected from a contemporary instrument.

Legacy ’54 Models

The Legacy ’54 Series reflects the bold aesthetics and refined tonal character of banjos from the 1950s.
These instruments combine strong projection with warmth and balance, producing a powerful tone suited for both stage and studio. Their distinctive visual style reflects an era of innovation, while their construction ensures consistency, stability, and responsiveness.
The Legacy ’54 Series represents tradition refined through modern craftsmanship.
