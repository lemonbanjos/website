Legacy Series Banjos

The Lemon Legacy Series brings together my 1930s- and 1950s-inspired resonator banjos – faithful to the look and feel of the originals, with Lemon’s modern refinements in playability, reliability, and tone. Models are designated by their era and pattern, such as Legacy ’35 LB-3 or Legacy ’54 LB-250.

Legacy ’35 Models

These banjos are based on mid-1930s one-piece-flange Mastertone patterns — the classic prewar mahogany and maple workhorses that defined bluegrass banjo as we know it.
